Goal

Earn money through YouTube by publishing product review videos

Review products bought on Shopee, TikTok Shop, and other platforms. Start on YouTube, repurpose to TikTok and Facebook Reels once the workflow is established. Target 1 video per week with Claude handling scripts, SEO, and content calendars.

Channel Positioning (pick one)

💰 "Is it worth the money?"

Budget-conscious angle. Review cheap Shopee/TikTok finds and give a clear verdict. Works well for a Vietnamese audience who wants to avoid wasting money.

💎 "Hidden gems"

Focus on products that surprised you — positively or negatively. Builds curiosity and repeat viewers who want to discover deals before others.

🚫 "Don't buy before watching this"

Negative framing builds trust fast. Warning people off bad products is just as valuable as recommending good ones — often more shareable.

🎯 Niche-specific (recommended later)

Once you find which product category gets the most engagement, double down on it (gadgets, home goods, beauty, kitchen tools). Easier to grow a loyal audience.

Starting recommendation: Begin with "Is it worth the money?" — it's versatile, works for any product category, and the verdict format keeps videos structured and easy to script.

Video Structure (repeat every time)

1

Hook (0–10s)

Show the product + the result upfront. Example: "This $3 phone holder either breaks in a week or it's the best deal on Shopee — let's find out."

2

What it is + price + where to buy

Name the product, price in VND/USD, platform. Drop the affiliate link in description here.

3

Unboxing

Quick, visual. Show packaging, what's included, first look.

4

First impressions

Build quality, feel, smell, packaging quality vs expectations.

5

Testing (the core)

Actually use it. This is what viewers came for — don't rush this section.

6

Pros & Cons

Be honest. Even one real con builds more credibility than a perfect review.

7

Verdict + who it's for

"Worth it if you need X, skip it if you need Y." Clear, specific.

8

CTA

Link in description, subscribe, "tell me what to review next in the comments."

Monetization Roadmap

Fastest — start now
Shopee Affiliate + TikTok Shop Affiliate
Link the exact product in the description. Earn commission per sale. No follower threshold required — you can earn from video #1. This is your primary income source before platforms unlock ad revenue.
Medium term — 3–6 months
YouTube Partner Program
Requires 1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours (or Shorts: 1,000 subs + 10M Short views in 90 days). Unlocks ad revenue on all videos.
Medium term
TikTok Creator Rewards Program
Pays based on views for videos 1 min+. Eligibility: 10,000 followers + 100,000 views in 30 days.
Medium term
Facebook In-Stream Ads / Reels Bonuses
Available once you hit Facebook's eligibility thresholds. Repost your YouTube content here for passive extra income.
Long term — once you have an audience
Brand Sponsorships
Sellers pay you directly to review their product. Even a small, niche audience commands rates — 10,000 engaged subs beats 100,000 passive ones for sponsors.

Equipment (minimal to start)

📱 Camera

Your phone is enough. Good lighting matters more than camera quality at this stage.

Have it already

💡 Lighting

Natural window light is free and works well. A cheap ring light (~$10–20) if you want consistency.

Optional upgrade

🎙 Microphone

A basic lapel mic (~$10–15). Audio quality affects retention more than video quality — worth getting early.

Buy soon
Key principle: Consistency matters more than production quality. A simple voiceover review posted weekly for 3 months will usually outperform a "perfect" video posted once.
